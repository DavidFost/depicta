honest work.

A one-person web studio based in Davenport, FL — working directly with small businesses and local organizations that deserve better than the agency experience.

Why Depicta Exists

Most small businesses get a bad deal on their website.

The standard options are an agency that charges thousands upfront and hands you a site you can't maintain, a freelancer who disappears after launch, or a drag-and-drop builder that looks fine until it doesn't.

Depicta was built to fill the gap: a professionally designed and fully managed website, with a real person handling everything, for one predictable monthly rate. No setup fees, no hidden costs, no waiting on a ticket system.

We work with small businesses, healthcare clinics, churches, and local service providers who need their website to work — and who want someone they can actually reach when something needs to change.

What Depicta Is Not

Not an agency. No account managers, no markups, no overhead passed to you.
Not a template shop. Every site is designed for your business specifically.
Not a platform. You won't log in to update your own site unless you want to.
Not a one-and-done freelancer. Ongoing support is part of every plan.

A Note from David

You'll work with me — not a team, not a system.

I'm David, and I run Depicta out of Davenport, FL. I started this because I kept seeing the same story: a small business owner pays too much for a site they can't update, or signs up for a platform they don't understand, or hires someone who disappears after the launch email.

Depicta is different by design. When you reach out, you're talking to me. When your site needs an update, you email me. When something breaks, I fix it. There's no ticket queue, no account manager, no one who has to "loop in the team." It's just me, focused on making sure your website works the way it should.

I work with businesses I'd genuinely recommend to someone I know — small shops, clinics, churches, and service providers that need a real web presence without the cost or complexity of an agency. If that sounds like you, I'd like to hear about your business.

How We're Different

What working with Depicta actually looks like.

No setup fees. Ever.

Your first month covers everything — design, build, domain, and launch. There's no deposit, no upfront cost, and no bill before your site is live.

One point of contact.

You'll always be working with David directly. No handoffs, no new faces every few months, no explaining your business to someone who doesn't know it.

Live in 2–3 weeks.

Most sites launch within two to three weeks of our first conversation. We move fast because your time has value — and because a website sitting unbuilt isn't helping anyone.

No lock-in after your minimum.

Short minimums of 3–6 months, then month-to-month. You stay because the service is worth it, not because you're stuck in a contract.

Updates are included.

Your plan includes monthly content updates — hours, prices, team changes, new photos. Send it over and it's done, usually the same day.

Flat rate. No surprises.

What you pay is what's agreed. No scope creep charges, no add-on fees for basic requests, no invoice that makes you feel like you need a lawyer to read it.
